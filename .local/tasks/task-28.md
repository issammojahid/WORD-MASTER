# Masterpiece Arabic Word Game Backgrounds

## What & Why
Replace the current cartoon backgrounds with premium professional art that feels like a true masterpiece and is deeply connected to the game "حروف المغرب" (Huruf Al-Maghrib — Letters of the Maghreb). The art must combine Moroccan cultural heritage with the game's neon color palette (#00F5FF cyan, #FF006E pink, #BF00FF purple, #F5C842 gold, #00FF87 green) on deep dark backgrounds, resulting in a world-class Arabic mobile word game aesthetic.

## Done looks like
- All 23 `bg_*.png` files replaced with premium, masterpiece-quality art
- Style: dark deep backgrounds (near-black navy/purple) with glowing neon accents in the game's palette — Arabic calligraphy as decorative art, Moroccan zellige tile patterns with neon glow, Moroccan arches and lanterns, ornate arabesque geometry
- Each screen background is thematically and visually unique — the game screen has glowing Arabic letter tiles, the home screen has the Moroccan night sky with Arabic letters, the shop has glowing treasure and gems, etc.
- The art feels like a premium award-winning Arabic mobile game (not generic or cartoon)
- No text artifacts, no English, no watermarks — pure atmospheric visual art
- The 3 popup backgrounds have ornate Moroccan frame designs that work as card backdrops

## Out of scope
- No TypeScript/JSX code changes (images swapped in place with same filenames)
- No layout or overlay changes
- No sound or animation changes

## Tasks

1. **Generate home, game, lobby, and rapid backgrounds** — These core gameplay screens need the strongest visual impact. bg_home: night sky over Moroccan medina silhouette, floating glowing Arabic letters ب ح م ر (the game's letters), neon cyan and gold light rays. bg_game: abstract mosaic of glowing Arabic letter tiles in the game's colors on dark background. bg_lobby: Moroccan arch doorway with glowing neon lights beyond, waiting theme. bg_rapid: electric storm over Moroccan geometric patterns, lightning and speed.

2. **Generate word-chain, daily, AI, and settings backgrounds** — bg_word_chain: glowing arabesque chain vine pattern. bg_daily: sunrise over Moroccan city skyline, calendar/crescent. bg_ai: digital Arabic calligraphy with circuit patterns, futuristic Moroccan. bg_settings: ornate Moroccan door with mechanical gears in gold neon.

3. **Generate shop, spin, VIP, and friends backgrounds** — bg_shop: Moroccan souk market with glowing gem and coin light, treasure aesthetic. bg_spin: glowing fortune wheel with Arabic geometric segments. bg_vip: royal Moroccan throne room, gold crown with purple neon glow. bg_friends: Moroccan lanterns hanging, warm neon glow connecting people.

4. **Generate achievements, battle_pass, tasks, and clans backgrounds** — bg_achievements: golden trophy among Moroccan stars and constellation patterns. bg_battle_pass: warrior armor silhouette with Moroccan shield design, epic purple sky. bg_tasks: scroll parchment with Moroccan border patterns. bg_clans: multiple shields with clan emblems, Moroccan fortress battlements.

5. **Generate leaderboard, league, tournament, and spectate backgrounds** — bg_leaderboard: podium on Moroccan marble floor, spotlights, gold silver bronze neon glow. bg_league: Arabic geometric league emblem, progression tiers with Moroccan tile steps. bg_tournament: championship arena with Moroccan arches framing, grand trophy. bg_spectate: Moroccan theater stage with velvet curtains, spotlight, audience silhouette.

6. **Generate popup backgrounds (3 images)** — bg_popup: ornate Moroccan frame border on deep dark background with subtle zellige texture. bg_popup_confirm: warm amber Moroccan arch frame with geometric border. bg_popup_reward: golden Moroccan star burst frame with gems and coins, celebration.
